What We Prevent

The 8 reasons AI creative fails before it ever gets published.

Most AI video problems aren't technical. They're creative and process failures. Here's what we find — and eliminate.

✕

Generic AI Face Syndrome

Every avatar looks the same. Audiences clock it instantly and trust drops. Brand differentiation disappears into the uncanny valley.

✓
How We Fix This

We build custom brand-specific avatars with locked visual identities — unique enough to own, consistent enough to scale.

✕

Audio–Video Sync Failures

Lips move out of time. Voiceover drifts. The result looks unfinished and undermines credibility in the first 3 seconds.

✓
How We Fix This

Every video goes through sync QA before delivery — audio aligned frame by frame, no drift, no mismatch.

✕

Off-Brand Color Rendering

AI ignores your brand palette and generates whatever looks good to the model. Your campaign looks like everyone else's.

✓
How We Fix This

Brand colors, fonts, and visual identity are locked into every generation brief — enforced at prompt level, not corrected after.

✕

Aspect Ratio Mismatches

A 16:9 video uploaded to Reels. A square ad served on YouTube. Wrong format, wrong platform, wasted spend.

✓
How We Fix This

Every asset is delivered in all required formats — 9:16, 16:9, and 1:1 — cut and checked before it leaves.

✕

Prompt Drift Across Variations

Each generated variant looks slightly different. The campaign loses visual coherence and the brand feels inconsistent.

✓
How We Fix This

We lock a master style prompt for every campaign — all variants generated from the same creative foundation.

✕

Uncanny Valley Avatars

Realistic enough to notice it's wrong. Not real enough to trust. The presenter kills the message before it lands.

✓
How We Fix This

We select and test avatar styles against brand and audience before any campaign asset is generated at scale.

✕

Platform Rejection

Wrong codec, wrong file size, wrong spec. The ad gets rejected at upload and the launch window closes.

✓
How We Fix This

Every export is checked against platform specs — Meta, TikTok, YouTube — before delivery. No rejections, no delays.

✕

No Creative Direction = Visual Noise

Vague prompts produce vague results. Without direction, AI generates impressive-looking content that says nothing.

✓
How We Fix This

Every brief goes through creative direction before generation starts — clear objectives, clear visual style, clear message.
