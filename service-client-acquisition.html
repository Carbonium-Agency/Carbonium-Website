What We Prevent

The 8 reasons acquisition systems fail

Most acquisition systems don't fail from lack of effort. They fail from the wrong structure. Here's what we watch for — and eliminate.

✕

No ICP defined

"We serve everyone" means every campaign misses. Without a defined ideal client, budget burns on the wrong people.

✓
How We Fix This

We build your ICP from CRM data and real buyer interviews — firmographics, psychographics, and buying triggers mapped before a single campaign runs.

✕

Campaigns, not systems

One-off effort produces feast-or-famine results. Without a repeatable system, you restart from zero every quarter.

✓
How We Fix This

We architect a full acquisition system — funnel, nurture, onboarding, and retention — that runs automatically without restarting from zero every quarter.

✕

Stopping at the sale

Poor onboarding causes early churn that destroys lifetime value before it ever compounds.

✓
How We Fix This

We design your onboarding journey to deliver first value within 30 days, reducing early churn before it compounds into lost lifetime value.

✕

Vanity metrics

High MQL volume with low SQL conversion means you're counting activity, not revenue. The wrong number kills strategy.

✓
How We Fix This

We set revenue-connected KPIs from day one — CAC, CLV, and SQL conversion rate — so every metric ties directly to pipeline, not activity.

✕

No lead scoring

Sales wastes time on low-intent prospects while high-value leads go cold. Prioritisation is everything.

✓
How We Fix This

We build an AI lead scoring model that ranks every prospect by intent and fit — sales only touches leads worth their time.

✕

Marketing and sales silos

When both teams define "qualified lead" differently, nothing converts cleanly. Alignment is non-negotiable.

✓
How We Fix This

We align both teams around a single MQL/SQL definition, shared dashboard, and unified lead handoff process — no more lost leads between teams.

✕

No feedback loop

Campaigns never improve because nobody measures what failed. Optimisation without data is just guessing.

✓
How We Fix This

We install a reporting cadence with monthly performance reviews — every campaign feeds data back into the next one automatically.

✕

Neglecting existing clients

LTV growth from retained clients is faster and cheaper than new acquisition. Most agencies ignore this entirely.

✓
How We Fix This

We build retention and upsell automation that grows LTV from existing clients — your cheapest and fastest revenue source, fully systematised.
