Failure Modes

Eight content mistakes. We prevent all of them.

Most AI content fails before it publishes. Here's what we watch for — and eliminate.

✕

Generic Output

Publishing AI content that sounds like every other brand in your category.

✓
How We Fix This

We lock your brand voice before a single word is generated — every piece passes a tone accuracy check.

✕

No Brand Voice

AI tools left unsupervised produce content with zero personality or differentiation.

✓
How We Fix This

We build a Brand Voice Document from your existing content, interviews, and messaging — then train our engine on it.

✕

Content Without Strategy

Producing volume with no topic plan, keyword intent, or audience mapping.

✓
How We Fix This

Every piece we produce is mapped to a content strategy, keyword cluster, and channel plan before writing begins.

✕

No Human Review

Publishing raw AI output without a human editor checking accuracy, tone, and quality.

✓
How We Fix This

Every piece goes through a human brand-voice and accuracy review before it reaches you — nothing ships unchecked.

✕

Hallucinated Facts

AI confidently writing statistics, claims, and references that are completely fabricated.

✓
How We Fix This

We fact-check every claim, stat, and reference in every piece before delivery — zero tolerance for misinformation.

✕

Format Mismatch

Writing blog-style content for social, or social-style content for email — wrong format, wrong channel.

✓
How We Fix This

Every piece is written natively for its platform — we never copy-paste across formats, we rebuild for each channel.

✕

Inconsistent Volume

Bursting with content one month then going silent — audiences disengage and algorithms penalise.

✓
How We Fix This

We deliver a fixed monthly content batch on a locked schedule — consistent output, every single month, no gaps.

✕

No Repurposing

Writing one piece and leaving it to die instead of turning it into a full content library.

✓
How We Fix This

Every long-form asset is automatically repurposed into 5+ formats — blog becomes social, email, ad, and script.
