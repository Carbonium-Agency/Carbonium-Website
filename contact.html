Common Questions

How much does a strategy call cost?

Nothing. A 30-minute strategy call with Carbonium is completely free. We'll give you real, actionable insights about your marketing regardless of whether we work together.

What happens after the strategy call?

At the end of the call, if we think we can help and you'd like to explore working together, we'll propose a specific plan. There's no obligation — if the timing isn't right, we'll tell you honestly and leave you better informed.

Do you work with businesses outside the region?

Yes. We work with businesses across multiple markets and with international brands looking to enter new markets. Get in touch to discuss your specific situation.

How quickly can you get started?

Depending on the service and scope, most Carbonium engagements begin within 2–3 weeks of signing. Some AI automation projects can be scoped and started within a week.
