## Design Direction

**Theme:** Futuristic dark — black background with electric blue and neon cyan accents, holographic feel
**Mood:** Premium, cutting-edge, confident, tech-forward
**Key Visual Elements:**
- Deep black (#000000 / near-black) base backgrounds
- Electric blue (#0066FF range) and neon cyan (#00FFFF / #00E5FF) accents
- Holographic gradient overlays and glassmorphism cards
- Animated AI/particle visuals in the hero section
- Smooth scroll-triggered entrance animations (fade, slide, glow)
- Interactive hover effects on service cards, buttons, and nav
- Subtle grid or circuit-board texture motifs
- Bold, modern sans-serif typography (e.g., Space Grotesk, Syne, or Inter)
