The Founder

Meet Sutriano Wu

Sutriano Wu has spent more than two decades building brands, running campaigns, and generating results for businesses across multiple industries and markets. From early-stage startups to established regional enterprises, he has seen what works, what doesn't, and what separates the businesses that grow from the ones that plateau.

Over 20 years in marketing teaches you one thing above all else: the tools change constantly, but the fundamentals remain the same. You need the right message, the right audience, and a relentless focus on what actually drives revenue.

What has changed — fundamentally and permanently — is the capability available to marketers willing to embrace it. Artificial intelligence has made it possible to do in days what once took months. To test in hours what once took quarters. To reach, qualify, and convert leads at a scale that would have required entire teams just five years ago.

Carbonium was founded to make that capability accessible to the businesses that need it most — ambitious owners and executives who want to use AI as a genuine competitive advantage, not just another marketing buzzword.

"Most agencies are still marketing like it's 2018. AI isn't the future of marketing anymore — it's the present. The businesses that adopt it now will dominate their categories within two years. The ones that don't will be playing catch-up."
— Sutriano Wu, Founder, Carbonium
